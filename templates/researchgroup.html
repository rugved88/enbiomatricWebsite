Lead Investigators

Dr. Sanchita Bandyopadhyay-Ghosh is currently working as a Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Manipal University Jaipur. She obtained her PhD from the University of Sheffield, U.K. with ‘Overseas Research Scholarship’. Before joining Sheffield, she was awarded Pre-doctoral Fellowship from University of Leuven, Belgium. Following her PhD, she worked as a Teaching Research Fellow at Alfred University, USA. She later joined University of Toronto, Canada as a Post-doctoral Research Fellow. Following her return to India, she joined Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani and later at Manipal University Jaipur. Dr. Sanchita Bandyopadhyay-Ghosh has secured research grants from various funding agencies and initiated several research programs on development of high performance biomedical materials including injectable bone implants, tunable hydrogels, bioactive glass-ceramics and 3D printed bio-nanocomposite implants.

Dr. Subrata Bandhu Ghosh is working as a professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Manipal University Jaipur. He pursued his PhD from the University of Sheffield, U.K. Before, joining Sheffield, he was awarded Pre-doctoral Fellowship from University of Leuven, Belgium. Upon completion of his PhD, Dr. Ghosh joined the University of Toronto, Canada as a Post-doctoral Fellow and later as a Research Scientist. Following his return to India, Dr. Ghosh joined BITS Pilani and later Manipal University Jaipur. Dr. Ghosh has initiated several research programs on sustainable additive manufacturing, microcellular bioplastics and biobased nanocomposites. Additionally, he served as the founding Director (Research) at Manipal University Jaipur.
